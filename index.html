Hamiltonian

  • Spin-orbit coupling
  • Tight binding model
  • \(d_z, d_{xy}, d_{x^2 - y^2}\)

$$ H_0 = a t \left(τ k_x σ_x + k_y σ_y \right) ⊗ I_2 + \frac{Δ}{2} σ_z ⊗ I_2 - λ τ \left(σ_z - 1 \right) ⊗ S_z$$

$$ H_0 = \left[ \begin{matrix} \dfrac{Δ}{2} & a t \left( τ k_x - i k_y \right) \\ a t \left( τ k_x + i k_y \right) & λ τ σ - \dfrac{Δ}{2} \end{matrix} \right] $$

Energy Bands

  • \(Δ\) Band splitting
  • \(λ\) Spin splitting
