  • \(\mathrm{MoS_2}\), \(\mathrm{WS_2}\), \(\mathrm{MoSe_2}\), \(\mathrm{WSe_2}\)
  • Similar to monolayer graphene: two inequivalent valleys: \(\vect{K}\), \(\vect{K}'\)
  • Strong spin-orbit coupling
  • Inversion symmetry breaking
  • Opposite valley Berry curvature
  • Tight binding model: \(d_z, d_{xy}, d_{x^2 - y^2}\)
{% slide div %}

$$ H_0^{τ σ} \exOfK = a t \left(τ k_x σ_x + k_y σ_y \right) ⊗ I_2 + \frac{Δ}{2} σ_z ⊗ I_2 - λ τ \left(σ_z - 1 \right) ⊗ S_z $$

$$ H_0^{τ σ} \exOfK = \left[ \begin{matrix} \dfrac{Δ}{2} & a t \left( τ k_x - i k_y \right) \\ a t \left( τ k_x + i k_y \right) & λ τ σ - \dfrac{Δ}{2} \end{matrix} \right] $$

  • \(τ\)—valley index
  • \(σ\)—spin index

  • \(μ = -0.83 \: \text{eV}\)

$$ E_{τ σ}^n \exOfK = \frac{1}{2} \left( λ τ σ + n \sqrt{ (2 a t)^2 \left\lvert \vect{k} \right\rvert^2 + \left( Δ - λ τ σ \right)^2 } \right) $$

{% endslide %} {% slide : Intrinsic Superconductivity %}

Density-density interaction

$$ \begin{align} H_V = - \frac{1}{2} \sideset{}{'}∑_{\substack{\vK, \vK' \\ \vect{q}}} \sideset{}{}∑_{\substack{ν, σ \\ ν', σ'}} \sideset{}{'}∑_{\substack{τ, \bar{τ} \\ τ', \bar{τ}'}} v \left( \vect{q} + \left( \bar{τ} - τ \right) \vK \right) × {a^ν_{\bar{τ} σ}}^† \left( \vK + \vect{q} \right) {a^{ν'}_{\bar{τ}' σ'}}^† \left( \vK' - \vect{q} \right) a^{ν'}_{τ' σ'} \left( \vK' \right) a^ν_{τ σ} \left( \vK \right) \end{align} $$

  • \(a^ν_{τ σ}\)—orbital operators
  • \(c^n_{τ σ}\)—unperturbed eigenstate operators
  • Project to the \(n = -1\), \(τ = σ\) band
  • Intra and inter valley scattering terms
  • Next step: mean-field analysis

$$ \begin{align} H_V = - \frac{1}{2} \sideset{}{'}∑_{\vK, \vK'} v \left( \vK' - \vK \right) × & B^2 \left( \vK, \vK' \right) \exInteration{+ ↑}{+ ↑} \\ + & B^2 \left( \vK', \vK \right) \exInteration{- ↓}{- ↓} \\ + & \left\lvert B \left( \vK, \vK' \right) \right\rvert^2 \left[ \exInteration{+ ↑}{- ↓} \\ + \exInteration{- ↓}{+ ↑} \right] , \end{align} $$

{% endslide %} {% slide : Induced Superconductivity %}

Intervalley pairing

  • \(a^ν_{τ σ}\)—orbital operators
  • \(b_α\)—quasiparticle operators
  • BCS pairs in opposite valleys
  • \(α = τ = σ\)
  • Reduces to standard BCS Hamiltonian where \(α\) plays the role of the spin index

$$ \begin{equation} H_V = - \sideset{}{'}∑_{\vK} \sideset{}{}∑_{ν, τ} Δ_ν {a^ν_{-τ ↓}}^† \exOfMK {a^ν_{τ ↑}}^† \exOfK + \hc \end{equation} $$

$$ \begin{equation} H - μ N = \sideset{}{'}∑_{\vK} \sideset{}{}∑_α λ_{\vK}^α b_{\vK α}^† b_{\vK α} + \sideset{}{'}∑_{\vK} \left(ξ_{\vK ↓} + λ_{\vK}^- \right) . \end{equation} $$

{% endslide %} {% slide : Optical Transitions %}

\(\left\lvert \vect{P}^{τ σ} \exOfK \right\rvert^2 = \left( m_0 / ħ \right)^2 \left\lvert \left\langle u_+ \right\rvert ∇_{\vK} H_0^{τ σ} \exOfK \left\lvert u_- \right\rangle \right\rvert^2\)

\(P_±^{τ σ} \exOfK = P_x^{τ σ} ± i P_y^{τ σ}\)

  • Right circular polarization strongly couples to \(τ = +\) valley transitions
  • Left circular polarization strongly couples to \(τ = -\) valley transitions

{% reference PhysRevLett.108.196802 %}

\(P_± \exOfK = P_x ± i P_y\)

\(\left\lvert Ω_f \right\rangle = \begin{cases} {c^+_α}^† \exOfK b_{-α} \exOfMK \left\lvert Ω \right\rangle & k > k_μ \\ {c^+_α}^† \exOfK b_{-α}^† \exOfMK \left\lvert Ω \right\rangle & k < k_μ \end{cases}\)

\(\left\lvert Ω \right\rangle = ∏_{\vK} b_{\vK ↑} b_{-\vK ↓} \left\lvert 0 \right\rangle\)
