{% endslide %} {% slide : Hamiltonian %}
  • Similar to monolayer graphene: two inequivalent valleys
  • Strong spin-orbit coupling
  • Inversion symmetry breaking
  • Opposite valley Berry curvature
  • Tight binding model
  • \(d_z, d_{xy}, d_{x^2 - y^2}\)

$$ H_0^{τ σ} \exOfK = a t \left(τ k_x σ_x + k_y σ_y \right) ⊗ I_2 + \frac{Δ}{2} σ_z ⊗ I_2 - λ τ \left(σ_z - 1 \right) ⊗ S_z $$

$$ H_0^{τ σ} \exOfK = \left[ \begin{matrix} \dfrac{Δ}{2} & a t \left( τ k_x - i k_y \right) \\ a t \left( τ k_x + i k_y \right) & λ τ σ - \dfrac{Δ}{2} \end{matrix} \right] $$

{% endslide %} {% slide : Energy Bands %}
  • \(Δ\) Band splitting
  • \(λ\) Spin splitting

$$ E_{τ σ}^n \exOfK = \frac{1}{2} \left( λ τ σ + n \sqrt{ (2 a t)^2 \left\lvert \vect{k} \right\rvert^2 + \left( Δ - λ τ σ \right)^2 } \right) $$

{% endslide %} {% slide : Intrinsic Superconductivity %}

Density-density interaction

$$ \begin{align} H_V = - \frac{1}{2} \sideset{}{'}∑_{\substack{\vK, \vK' \\ \vect{q}}} \sideset{}{}∑_{\substack{ν, σ \\ ν', σ'}} \sideset{}{'}∑_{\substack{τ, \bar{τ} \\ τ', \bar{τ}'}} v \left( \vect{q} + \left( \bar{τ} - τ \right) \vK \right) × {a^ν_{\bar{τ} σ}}^† \left( \vK + \vect{q} \right) {a^{ν'}_{\bar{τ}' σ'}}^† \left( \vK' - \vect{q} \right) a^{ν'}_{τ' σ'} \left( \vK' \right) a^ν_{τ σ} \left( \vK \right) \end{align} $$

Projecting to the lower band

  • Project to the upper \(n = -1\) bands \(τ = σ\)
  • Intra and inter valley scattering terms

$$ \begin{align} H_V = - \frac{1}{2} \sideset{}{'}∑_{\vK, \vK'} v \left( \vK' - \vK \right) × & B^2 \left( \vK, \vK' \right) \exInteration{+ ↑}{+ ↑} \\ + & B^2 \left( \vK', \vK \right) \exInteration{- ↓}{- ↓} \\ + & \left\lvert B \left( \vK, \vK' \right) \right\rvert^2 \left[ \exInteration{+ ↑}{- ↓} \\ + \exInteration{- ↓}{+ ↑} \right] , \end{align} $$
